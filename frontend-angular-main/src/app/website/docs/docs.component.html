How to start Shopping

To get started, you need to do the following:

  1. Login: Enter your email & password for login
  2. Navigation: Use the navigation bar to explore different sections of the website.
  3. Product Showcase: Browse through the featured products on the home page.
  4. View Products: Browse through the products listed on the website.
  5. Add to Cart: Add desired items to the shopping cart by clicking the "Add to Cart" button on the product detail page.
  6. Remove Items: Remove items from the cart if needed.
  7. Place Order: Click on the "Order" button to proceed to the place order.
  8. Order History: View your past orders and their statuses.

How to Manage Platform

To get started, you need to do the following:

  1. Login: Enter your (admin) email & password for login
  2. User Management: View and manage user accounts, including deletion
  3. Product Management: View and delete products from the inventory.
  4. Order Management: Monitor orders and view order history.
